# Amenities

The Amenities section manages bookable facilities within the building. Accessible via sidebar or top navigation.

**URL**: `/search-amenities`

## Page Header

- **Title**: "Amenities" (with trophy icon)
- **View All Bookings**: Button to see all current bookings
- **Check Combined Calendar**: Button to view combined calendar across amenities
- **Building Selector**: Dropdown to filter by building (e.g., "Bond")

## Amenity Cards

Each amenity is displayed as a card with:
- **Photo**: Image of the amenity
- **Name**: Amenity name (clickable link)
- **Instruction Message**: Booking rules and guidelines
- **Booking Fee**: Cost to book
- **Damage Deposit Fee**: Refundable deposit amount
- **Book Button**: To make a reservation
- **View Button**: To see details/calendar

## Available Amenities (Bond Building)

### 1. BBQ Bond
- Max guests: 4 per reservation
- Time limit: 2 hours
- Hours: 11 AM - 11 PM
- Time slots: 6 slots (11:00-13:00, 13:00-15:00, 15:00-17:00, 17:00-19:00, 19:00-21:00, 21:00-23:00)
- Booking Fee: $0
- Damage Deposit: $0

### 2. BBQ 2 Bond
- Same rules as BBQ Bond
- Max guests: 4
- Time limit: 2 hours
- 6 time slots from 11 AM to 11 PM
- Booking Fee: $0
- Damage Deposit: $0

### 3. Billiard Room Bond
- Max guests: 4
- Reservation times: 2 hours limit
- Hours: 11 AM - 11 PM
- 6 time slots
- Booking Fee: $0
- Damage Deposit: $0

### 4. Elevator Bond
- Booking time slots vary by day:
  - Monday to Friday: 3 slots (9am-12pm, 1pm-4pm, 6pm-9pm)
  - Saturday: 2 slots (9am-12pm, 1pm-4pm)
  - Sunday and holidays: No bookings allowed
- Elevator rental fee: $50.00 (for move-in, move-out, and delivery)
- Damage deposit required prior to booking
- Fee structure:
  - Move-In Related Booking Fee: $50
  - Move-Out Related Booking Fee: $50
  - Delivery Related Booking Fee: $50
  - Move-In Related Damage Deposit: $250
  - Move-Out Related Damage Deposit: $500
  - Delivery Related Damage Deposit: $250
- Payment: Cash or E-transfer accepted

### 5. Guest Suite Bond
- Booking Fee: $200
- Damage Deposit Fee: $250
- Payment: Cash or E-transfer accepted

### 6. Party Room Bond
- Security guard required for events with 11+ people
- Events with 10 or fewer: no security guard needed
- Security guard rate: $106.20 + HST = $120.00 (minimum 4 hours)
- Additional hours: $26.55 + HST per hour
- Premium rate if payment not received within 24 hours: $52.50/hour
- Payment: Personal or certified checks payable to TSCC 2584, or cash/E-transfer
- Booking Fee: $120
- Damage Deposit Fee: $1,000

### 7. Golf and Video Game Room
- Agreement must be signed and handed to Concierge
- Max guests: 4
- Time limit: 2 hours
- Hours: 11 AM - 11 PM
- 6 time slots (same as BBQ)
- Booking Fee: $0
- Damage Deposit: $0

## Key Features
- Building-specific amenity filtering
- Photo galleries for each amenity
- Detailed booking instructions per amenity
- Variable fee structures (booking fees + damage deposits)
- Time slot-based booking system
- Combined calendar view across all amenities
